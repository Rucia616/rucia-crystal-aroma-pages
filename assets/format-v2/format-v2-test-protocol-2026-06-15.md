## Objective

Validate which product format is most worth pursuing before Rucia spends on samples, packaging, ads, or creator seeding.

## Core Decision

Should the first validated offer be:

- A: Crystal Aroma Stone Kit
- B: Reed Room Diffuser
- C: Full Room Mood Set

## Why Use Render-Style Mockups

Use render-style product mockups for this test.

Pure concept boards are too abstract for product-form validation. Photorealistic AI product renders can create false confidence and may introduce illegible labels or impossible product details.

This asset uses controlled render-style mockups so respondents can judge the product form while text, price, contents, and scent expectations stay readable.

## Test Design

- Same scent across all options: Clean Reset
- Same VI across all options
- Same image quality across all options
- A and B are both $49 to compare format preference
- C is $79 to test whether a full gift set can justify a higher order value

## Recommended Audience

- US-based women
- Ages 18-34
- Optional targeting: home decor, fragrance, wellness, gifts, TikTok/Pinterest-style discovery

## Primary Question

Which option would you be most likely to click on or consider buying if you saw it in an Instagram/TikTok ad, gift guide, or online shop?

Ask respondents to explain:

- What they think the product is
- What feels appealing
- What feels confusing or not believable
- Whether the price feels reasonable

## Pass Gates

Continue if:

- A, B, or C has a clear win on click intent or purchase consideration.
- At least 65% correctly identify the products as home fragrance / room scent / diffuser formats.
- Stone Kit or Full Set does not materially lose on scent credibility.
- Price rejection is fixable by contents, bundle clarity, or value framing.

## Stop / Revise Gates

Revise before spending more if:

- A is understood mostly as crystal decor.
- B wins only because A and C look confusing.
- C is seen as cluttered, overpriced, or hard to use.
- Users doubt scent strength for Stone Kit and Full Set.
- Users anchor A/C below $30.

## Decision Rules

If A wins:

Prioritize Crystal Aroma Stone Kit sampling, but test scent throw before committing to packaging.

If B wins:

Keep reed diffuser as the performance format and use VI / quiz / scent sign to differentiate.

If C wins:

Explore Full Room Mood Set as a limited gift offer, then immediately model COGS, shipping, breakage, and packaging complexity.

If the result is flat:

Do not pick by vote count alone. Use these tie-breakers:

1. Correct product understanding
2. Scent credibility
3. Price acceptance
4. Giftability
5. Confusion risk

## Cash Rule

This test can justify a sample shortlist. It cannot justify MOQ, formal packaging, paid ads, or paid creator campaigns.
