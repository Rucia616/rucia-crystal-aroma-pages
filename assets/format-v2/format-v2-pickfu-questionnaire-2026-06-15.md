# Aroma Oracle Format V2 PickFu Questionnaire

Date: 2026-06-15
Asset: `format-v2-same-scent-3-way-test-2026-06-15.png`
Audience: US-based women, 18-34, interested in home decor / wellness / fragrance / gifts if targeting allows.

## Recommended Poll Type

Three-option image poll.

## Question

Which option would you be most likely to click on or consider buying if you saw it in an Instagram/TikTok ad, gift guide, or online shop?

Please choose one and explain:

- What do you think the product is?
- What feels most appealing?
- What feels confusing, weak, or not believable?
- Does the shown price feel reasonable for that option?

## Options

- A: Crystal Aroma Stone Kit, $49
- B: Reed Room Diffuser, $49
- C: Full Room Mood Set, $79

## Why This Test Exists

The previous PickFu concept poll tested language and visual direction. It did not prove which physical product format should launch.

This test isolates product form while keeping the scent and VI consistent.

## Success Signals

- At least 65% of respondents correctly identify all options as home fragrance / room scent / diffuser products.
- Stone Kit or Full Set must show a clear advantage in click appeal, giftability, uniqueness, or visual memory.
- Reed may win scent credibility; if so, keep it as a performance format or comparison anchor.
- Full Set must justify the $79 price through completeness, gifting, and clarity.

## Stop / Revise Signals

- Respondents think A is mostly decor, crystals, or a spiritual object rather than fragrance.
- Respondents say C looks cluttered, expensive, or confusing.
- Respondents say B is the only option that seems able to scent a room.
- Respondents anchor A or C to low-price diffuser/decor products under $30.
- Respondents do not understand the role of the quiz / scent sign card.

## What This Test Does Not Prove

- It does not prove scent liking.
- It does not prove actual scent throw.
- It does not prove supplier quality.
- It does not prove repeat purchase.
- It does not validate final packaging.

## Recommended Spend Rule

Run only after the image is visually approved.

Suggested first spend: $35-$70.

Do not buy samples, run ads, pay creators, or commit to MOQ from this test alone.
